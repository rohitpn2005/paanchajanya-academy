PAANCHAJANYA ACADEMY — HERO BACKGROUND VIDEOS
=============================================

Each page's landing (hero) section plays a looping, muted background video.
Replace these placeholder clips with your own footage, keeping the SAME name:

   home-hero.mp4        Home landing
   yoga-hero.mp4        Yoga landing
   champions-hero.mp4   House of Champions landing
   pytta-hero.mp4       PYTTA (table tennis) landing
   kids-hero.mp4        Kids landing
   workshops-hero.mp4   Workshops landing
   reviews-hero.mp4     Reviews landing
   contact-hero.mp4     Contact landing

Guidelines:
 - MP4 (H.264) for best browser support. 1280x720 or 1920x1080.
 - Keep them short (5 to 12 sec) and small (a few MB) so pages load fast.
 - They autoplay muted and loop. The poster frame is images/heroes/<page>.jpg.
 - If a video file is missing, the page falls back to the animated gradient,
   so the site never looks broken.
